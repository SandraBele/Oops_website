Frequently Asked Questions

When can I take the Oops test?

You can test from the first day of your missed period. If you test early and get a negative result, test again in a few days.

How accurate is it?

Oops pregnancy tests are over 99% accurate when used from the day you expect your period.

Are the results easy to read?

Yes! The test shows simple results. Follow the leaflet for details on interpreting the lines.
