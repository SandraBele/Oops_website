Pregnancy Test (Midstream)

Oops or not, or just a "let's double‑check" — buying a pregnancy test can feel awkward, especially if you're picking one up for a partner. That's why we've gone stealth with sleek black packaging. Your purchase stays between us, free from prying eyes.

When to Use: You can test from the first day of a missed period and any day thereafter.
How to Use:
  1. Collect a small amount of urine in a clean, dry container.
  2. Place approximately 4 drops of urine onto the test circle and lay the cassette flat.
  3. Observe your results within 3–6 seconds.

Single use only. Store between 4°C and 30°C and keep out of reach of children.
